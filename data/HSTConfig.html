HST Settings

Calculate mVperAmp using: mVperAmp = max voltage range (mV) / current rating (A)

Examples:
• 20A CT rated at 2.5V ±0.625V → 625 mV / 20A = 31.25 mV/A
• 50A CT rated at 2.5V ±0.625V → 625 mV / 50A = 12.5 mV/A
• 100A CT rated at 2.5V ±0.625V → 625 mV / 100A = 6.25 mV/A
Subtracted from the DC current reading to zero out sensor bias. Connect the CT with no load, read the DC Current above, and enter that value here.

Wiring & Pin Reference

The HST analog output is read in differential mode through the ADS1x15 ADC. The sensor is not connected directly to any ESP32 GPIO.

• ADS AIN0 — HST signal (+)
• ADS AIN1 — HST reference (−)
The ADS chip talks to the ESP32 over I²C. These two pins are shared with all other I²C devices on the board.

• SDA — GPIO 21
• SCL — GPIO 22
